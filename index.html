About SmartRisk

An independent voice in a complex risk landscape

PT Solusi Manajemen Risiko Terintegrasi (SmartRisk) was established in March 2020 as a joint venture, 90% owned by YPK PLN and 10% by PT Solusi Manajemen Risiko Terpadu. We provide unbiased, expert-led risk consulting, free from insurer or broker conflicts of interest.

Headquartered in Jakarta and affiliated with YPK-PLN, we bring deep experience across Indonesia's energy, utilities, mining, and infrastructure sectors. Our team holds professional certifications across risk management, HSE, insurance, and technical inspection.

Our Services

Operational Risk Assessment

Systematic identification and evaluation of operational hazards including HAZOP studies and technical inspection.

Project Risk Assessment

End-to-end risk analysis for capital projects from feasibility through construction and commissioning.

Asset Valuation

Insurance & Risk Planning

Independent insurance program design, benchmarking, and placement advisory.

Business Continuity & Resilience

Business impact analysis, continuity planning, and disaster risk reduction strategies.
