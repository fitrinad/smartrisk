About SmartRisk

An independent voice in a complex risk landscape

PT Solusi Manajemen Risiko Terintegrasi (SmartRisk) was established in March 2020 as a joint venture, 90% owned by YPK PLN and 10% by PT Solusi Manajemen Risiko Terpadu (Smart). We provide unbiased, expert-led risk consulting, free from insurer or broker conflicts of interest.

Headquartered in Jakarta and affiliated with YPK-PLN, we bring deep experience across Indonesia's energy, utilities, mining, and infrastructure sectors. Our team holds professional certifications across risk management, HSE, insurance, and technical valuation.

Our Vision
To be the leading independent risk management consultancy trusted by energy and industrial clients across Indonesia and beyond.
Our Mission
To deliver rigorous, independent, and actionable risk assessments that protect people, assets, and the environment.

How We Work

The Interwoven Method

Our methodology integrates technical inspection, quantitative risk modeling, and insurance market knowledge into a seamless risk management process.

Each engagement is led by a certified risk professional and draws on a multidisciplinary team of engineers, HSE specialists, and insurance advisors.

1
Risk Identification
Systematic identification of hazards through site visits, document reviews, and stakeholder interviews.
2
Risk Analysis
Qualitative and quantitative assessment of likelihood and consequence for each identified risk.
3
Risk Evaluation
Prioritization against client risk appetite and regulatory thresholds.
4
Risk Treatment
Development of actionable mitigation recommendations and implementation roadmap.
5
Monitoring & Review
Ongoing review and reporting to track risk posture over time.
