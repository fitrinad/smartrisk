**SmartRisk** delivers BNSP-certified occupational Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) training and competency assessment programs. Our courses are designed for safety practitioners across Indonesia's industrial sectors.


## Training Programs & BNSP Certification

- General HSE (K3)
- Oil & Gas Safety (K3 Migas)
- Oil and Gas Safety Manager
- Authorized Gas Tester
- Safety Inspector
- HSE Management System (SMK3) Auditor
- Incident Investigation


## Skills & Awareness Training tailored to client needs

- Basic Firefighting or Fire Suppression Techniques
- HSE Leadership 
- Behavioral-Based Safety (BBS) 
- Implementation of the Contractor Safety Management System (CSMS) 
- Hazard Identification, Risk Assessment, and Determining Control (HIRADC) 
- Hazard and Operability Study (HAZOPs)
- HSE Planned Inspections
- Authorized Permit to Work (Gas Safety Inspector) 
- Emergency Preparedness & Response Plan
- HSE Training for Gas Station and LPG Station Managers
- Training for HSE Supervisors (Safety Officers) at Gas Stations and LPG Stations
