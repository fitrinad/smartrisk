The occurrence of a disaster can have a significant impact on industrial facilities and the surrounding community. Stakeholders must ensure that all elements are capable of delivering an appropriate emergency response.

**SmartRisk** offers a comprehensive solution for disaster risk reduction initiatives; providing systematic management of administrative decisions, organizational structures, operational skills, and capacity-building; to implement the policies, strategies, and coping capabilities required when a disaster strikes.
