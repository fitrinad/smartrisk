**SmartRisk** provides HSE consultation to develop and improve integrated HSE management systems tailored to each client's operational context.

## Scope of Our Service

- Development or improvement of integrated HSE management systems, including QHSE Management Systems (SMK3), and OHSAS Performance Evaluation
- Action plans for improvement to achieve PROPER environmental ratings
- Action plans for improvement of ISRS (International Sustainable Rating System) scores
- Development of internal company procedures for Contractor Safety Management System (CSMS)
- Development of emergency preparedness and emergency response plan (ERP) procedures
- Fire protection design planning
- Pre-fire planning support and emergency drill simulations
- Assistance in the execution of incident investigations (major incidents such as fire cases or fatality accidents)
