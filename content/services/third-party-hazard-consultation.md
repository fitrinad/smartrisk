Major incidents or accidents can place surrounding communities in dangerous situations. If not comprehensively addressed, third-party liability can arise from such events.

**SmartRisk** provides broad analysis of surrounding areas and proposes effective mitigation measures to reduce the impact of third-party hazards in the surrounding environment.
