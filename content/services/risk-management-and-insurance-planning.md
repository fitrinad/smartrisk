**SmartRisk** offers risk management and insurance planning services to help companies review their risk management strategies and risk-transfer measures. With their expertise in insurance, our engineers can coordinate between technical and finance departments regarding risk management measures and related insurance practices, enabling effective insurance program design aligned with organizational risk appetite.

## Scope of Our Service

- Review of existing risk management strategies
- Risk-transfer measure analysis and recommendations
- Insurance program design and structuring
- Coordination between technical and finance stakeholders
