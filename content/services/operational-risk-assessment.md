**SmartRisk** provides operational risk assessment services for various facilities, tailored specifically to the agreed-upon scope of work. In general, this service is useful for both existing and newly proposed insurance plans. The results of this risk assessment can be used to clearly identify and communicate a detailed risk profile of the assets.

## Scope of Our Service

- Identification of assets and natural hazards as well as operational hazards
- Analysis of facility condition, maintenance, and reliability
- Development of loss estimation scenarios including Estimated and Probable Maximum Loss (EML & PML)
- Business interruption risk analysis
- Risk assessment of assets using Geospatial Information System (GIS) data and relevant simulation software
