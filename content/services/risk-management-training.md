**SmartRisk** delivers comprehensive risk management training equipping participants with the knowledge and skills to implement and audit risk management systems across their organizations, with pathways to BNSP certification.

We can develop customized training programs with our experts, based on client needs. 

## Training Programs

- Training Needs Analysis
- Project, Program, and Portfolio Management
- Enterprise Risk Management Based on ISO 31000:2009, the International Standard for Risk Management
- Risk Management for Enterprise Asset Management
- Risk-Based Audit (RBA)
- Project Risk Management
- Business Continuity Management based on ISO 22301:2012
 

## BNSP Training & Certification

- Certified Risk Management Professional (CRMP)
- Certified Risk Management Officer (CRMO)
- Certified Government Risk Professional (CRGP)
- Certified Business Continuity Management Professional (BCMCP)
